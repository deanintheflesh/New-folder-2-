The Heroes

Main Characters

🤠

Dean Winchester

The Elder Hunter

The practical and fearless older brother. Wields the legendary Colt and drives his beloved 1967 Impala. His heart always belongs to family.

📚

Sam Winchester

The Intellectual

The smart and studious younger brother. Studied law before returning to hunting. Possesses mysterious psychic abilities tied to a dark destiny.

😇

Castiel

Angel of the Lord

A heavenly angel who pulls Dean from Hell and becomes an indispensable ally. His devotion to the brothers often puts him at odds with Heaven itself.

💀

Bobby Singer

The Mentor

A seasoned hunter and master researcher. The brothers' last resort in any crisis — a surrogate father who always answers the phone.

What Lurks in the Dark

🧛

Vampires

Organized in secret nests, they feed on human blood and are harder to kill than the legends suggest.

👻

Ghosts

Souls trapped between worlds, bound by violent deaths or powerful unfinished attachments.

😈

Demons

Human souls twisted in Hell, capable of possessing the living and serving Lucifer's dark agenda.

🐺

Werewolves

Cursed by a bite, they transform under the full moon and crave the hearts of the living.

🐉

Leviathans

Ancient beasts predating creation itself, released from Purgatory in Season Seven.

👁️

Angels

Heaven's soldiers — powerful, cold, and not always on the right side of the fight.
